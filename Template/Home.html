WE ARE

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) is one of the five departments at Nagarjuna College of Engineering and Technology. It administers bachelor's programs in Computer Science and Engineering, as well as master's and doctoral degree programs in Computer Science and Engineering.

About twenty full-time faculty members provide a comprehensive program that emphasizes problem solving skills in C programming, Web designing aspects, data structure and application level thinking through EBox Portal. Pattern recognition, data mining and machine learning, distributed systems and android application, operating systems and computer networks, computer security, and IOT computing.

The CSE department's many strengths include its high faculty-to-student ratio, state of the art facilities, renowned faculty, strong focus on undergraduate education balanced with leading-edge research, and emphasis on leadership, service, and ethics.

Faculty and students often collaborate on projects, In addition to the excellent faculty and quality research opportunities available through the department, there is a real sense of community and teamwork here.

The CSE department also offers three optional Concentrations for undergraduate majors: Music, Dance, and Photography. These concentrations provide students interested in those topic areas with a recommended sequence of elective courses. Completion of one of the Concentrations is recorded on the student's official transcript at graduation.

Graduates from the CSE department are heavily recruited by many top companies. Graduates take up careers in the computing industry, the finance industry, traditional large engineering companies, startup companies, management consulting companies, medical informatics companies, and government. Some of our top undergraduates each year enter the PhD program in Computer Science and Engineering at leading universities around the country.

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering department also offers PhD Doctoral Degree with many aspirants reaching heights in field of Research in various fields and also enriching their personal growth by Publishing National and International papers, National and International Journals and Research Patenting due to an excellent support from PhD Doctorate Guides.
